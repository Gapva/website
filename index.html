Super Mario 64 Coop Deluxe

What is Deluxe?

Coop Deluxe is a fork of sm64ex-coop with additional features and a more powerful Lua API, primarily developed by Agent X and AngelicMiracles.

What makes this different from EX-Coop?

Coop Deluxe has a massively improved ROM to executable patcher, additional multiplayer features, customization options galore, new built-in mods, and more!

How do I play Coop Deluxe?
